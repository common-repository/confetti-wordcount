Motivate yourself with a weekly overview of your word count stats on your dashboard and a realtime word count progress meter just below your editor.
== Description ==
**Total & Weekly Word Count Overview**

Get a quick overview of how much you've written weekly on your WordPress dashboard. See a summary of word count shown in weekly fashion. 

**Word Count Progress Meter **

Set a word count goal for your post and get a sense of how far you have written with a visual word count meter just below the text editor.  

**Confetti Shower Upon Reaching Word Count Goal**

Celebrate with confetti shower on the visual editor when you reach your word count goal.

You can learn more at [https://wpimager.com/confetti-wordcount/](https://wpimager.com/confetti-wordcount/)

== Installation ==
1. Upload confetti-wordcount to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress
3. Confetti Word Count can be seen on the WordPress Dashboard and just below the tinyMCE visual editor
